Our full range of STS smart meters.

Split prepaid meters, each in two parts: a Metering and Control Unit in the cabinet and a Customer Interface Unit with keypad in the home. Built to DLMS/COSEM and STS standards, with anti-tamper protection and remote management. Panel-mount for homes and businesses, DIN-rail for cabinets and sub-metering, each with a matching GFRP meter box. Every meter is Arotob-branded, made in China and assembled in Nigeria.
